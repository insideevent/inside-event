About Inside Event

Inside Event is a boutique nightlife and live-show producer based in Baghdad, crafting high-impact experiences that blend international talent, premium hospitality, and meticulous production design.

Since our founding, we have focused on elevating the region’s nightlife standard through disciplined planning, partnerships with world-class audio-visual teams, and a guest-first mindset. Our VIP model is built on reliability and detail: hosted tables, fast-track entry, and dedicated concierge support from purchase to final song.
